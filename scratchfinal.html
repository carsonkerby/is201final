The best stadium in college football

The best stadium in college football is of course Lavell Edwards Stadium located right here in Provo and home of the BYU cougars. With over 63,000 seats this place is always electric come game time!



The greatest college football programs of all time

It is hard to look at simply just one metric when considering the best college football teams of all time. So we are going to look at a couple of them.

  1. Most National Championships All Time
    • Yale (18)
    • Alabama (16)
    • Princeton (15)
    • Notre Dame (13)
    • Michigan (10)

  2. Most Wins All Time
    • Michigan (989)
    • Alabama (953)
    • Ohio State (953)
    • Notre Dame (938)
    • Texas (936)

  3. Most Heisman Trophy Winners All Time
    • Ohio State (7)
    • Oklahoma (7)
    • Notre Dame (7)
    • Southern California (7)
    • Alabama (4)
